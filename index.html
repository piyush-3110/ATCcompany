Fibre Glass Tissue

ATC Supplies Surface Tissue And Fiberglass Tissue (Also Known As RP Tissue).
ATC Fiberglass Tissue Is A Non Woven Felt Made From Randomly Oriented Glass Fibers Bound By A Resin In A Wet Process.
Fiberglass Tissue Is Used In The Following Application:
1. Waterproofing
2. Insulation Facing
3. Lining Of Acoustic Ducts
4. Pipe And Corrosion Protection
5. Special Grades Are Used For Battery Separators
6. FRP Manufacture
We Have The Following Grades Of Tissue Available In White And Black Color:
1. 35 GSM
2. 45 GSM
3. 100 GSM
Our ATC Fiberglass Tissue (RP Tissue) Is Available Through Distributors Across India.

Mineral Wood and Rock Wool

ATC Supplies Rock Wool Manufactured In India That Follows Relevant Indian Standards (IS 8183) And International Standards.
We Supply Roxul Brand And ATC Brand Rockwool. We Can Supply Plain Rockwool Or With Facings - FSK, Vinyl, Tissue Or Metal Mesh. Rockwool Is Used For Thermal (Hot And Cold) And For Acoustic Insulation. Rockwool Has Extremely Low Thermal Conductivity And It Can Be Used At A Very Wide Temperature Range (From -50OC To 750OC). Apart From This, ATC Rockwool Is Incombustible, Fire Retardant And Non Corrosive.
Thermal Insulation Of Tanks, Large Vessels, Steam And Other Pipes, Heat Exchangers , ESPs, Boilers, Cavity Walls, HVAC Ducts, AHU, Internal Duct Lining, , Roof Decks , And Other Commercial Building & Process Equipment Are Among Its Main Uses.
Rockwool Is Also Extensively Used For Acoustic Insulation For Movie Theaters, Studios, Generator Sheds And Other Sound Control Process.

Ceramic Fiber Blanket

Rosewool Ceramic Fiber Blanket Has Excellent Specialties Of Low Thermal Conductivity, Good Heat Stability, Thermal Shock Resistance And Tensile Strength, Good Thermal Insulation, Fire Proof And Noise Reduction.
